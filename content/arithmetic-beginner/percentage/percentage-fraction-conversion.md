## Conversion of Fraction into Percentage 

We know that: x as a percentage of y =  (x/y) √ó 100%

We already have a fraction, i.e. we already know ùë•/ùë¶ 

So, to convert a fraction into a percentage, multiply the fraction by 100 and put ‚Äò%‚Äô sign.

###### Q. Convert 1/2 into percentage
Explanation:<br>
<div class="Exp">
$\frac{1}{2}$ √ó 100 = 50%
</div>

###### Q. Convert 14/5 into percentage
Explanation:<br>
<div class="Exp">
$\frac{14}{5}$ = $\frac{10}{5}$ + $\frac{4}{5}$ = 2 + $\frac{4}{5}$ <br>
Now, (2 + $\frac{4}{5}$) √ó 100 = 200% + 80% = 280%
</div>

###### Q. Convert 11/12 into percentage
Explanation:<br>
<div class="Exp">
$\frac{11}{12}$ = 1 - $\frac{1}{12}$ <br>
Now, (1 - $\frac{1}{12}$) √ó 100 = 100% - 8 $\frac{1}{3}$ = (100 ‚Äì 9) + (1 - $\frac{1}{3}$) = 91 $\frac{2}{3}$ % <br>
(this method will be faster than directly multiplying the percentage value of 1/12 by 11)
</div>

## Conversion of Percentage into Fraction

We know that: x as a percentage of y  =  x/y √ó 100%

We already have the percentage. We just need to find out ùë•/ùë¶ 

So, to convert a percentage into a fraction, replace the ‚Äò%‚Äô sign with 1/100 and reduce the fraction to simplest form.

###### Q. Convert 20% into fraction
Explanation:<br>
<div class="Exp">
20% = 20/100 = 1/5
</div>

###### Q. Convert 166 $\frac{2}{3}$% into fraction
Explanation:<br>
<div class="Exp">
166 $\frac{2}{3}$% = 100% + 66 $\frac{2}{3}$% = 1 + 2/3 = 5/3
</div>

###### Q. Convert 457 $\frac{1}{7}$% into fraction
Explanation:<br>
<div class="Exp">
457 $\frac{1}{7}$% = 400% + 57 $\frac{1}{7}$% = 4 + 4/7 = 32/7
</div>
